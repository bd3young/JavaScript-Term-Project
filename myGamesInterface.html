My Games

Snake Recreation

This game is a recreation of the highly popular snake game. The goal of this game it so get your snake as long as possible by collecting the blue blocks and avoiding the walls and red blocks. You can control the snake with the arrow keys on your keyboard and the game will autimatically reset when you hit a wall or red block.

Drive Training

In this game you are a cop car on a highway. The objective of the game is to avoid the highway walls and earn the highest score possible. To control the cop car you will use the directional buttons directly below the game. If you hit a wall and you would like to replay the game just refresh your web browser.

Make a Pizza

In this game you get to add toppings to your pizza. The objective is to make your ideal pizza with the toppings given. To move the toppings or the pizza just move your cursor over the object you want to move. Click and hold the object and then move it to your desired location. Reapeat this process until you have the ideal pizza.
